**Dense Field** — noise texture shaped by a rhythmic speech-like envelope. **Triangular Decay** — amplitude narrowing to silence, the shape of every struck thing. **Granular Scatter** — pointillist dispersion, sound as particle cloud. **Lattice Decay** — cross-hatched structure dissolving under its own weight. **Pure Decay** — a clean sine wave dying exponentially, the most honest waveform. **Amplitude Pinch** — modulation squeezing signal through a zero crossing. **Reverse Build** — crescendo into density, time running backward. **Cross-Fade** — two signals passing through each other, neither dominant. **Burst Pattern** — rhythmic clusters with spatial decay, percussion rendered as geography.
